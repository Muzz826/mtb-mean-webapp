About the Author: Ben Muzzy

Hi! My name is Ben Muzzy and I am a lover of technology. I had worked in technology my whole adult career and absolutely loved it. Even with having Cerebral Palsy I was able to work in several areas of IT including helpdesk, desktop support, network administration, and even software development/quality assurance.

In 2019 though I was diagnosed with a second neurological condition called Functional Neurological Disorder. This condition has affected my life on so many different levels and the worst part is there is no cure.

My FND has gotten so bad I'm unable to perform required job duties at any company, so I decided to create this site to help spread my love of tech to all of you and hopefully help some of you with your tech problems.
